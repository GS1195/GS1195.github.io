The work

I'm a PhD researcher at the Institute of Physical Chemistry, University of Münster, with my thesis submitted. From 2022 to 2025 I worked within FestBatt-2, a BMBF-funded initiative advancing solid-state battery research from the atomistic scale up to industry.

My focus is the molecular modelling of highly conductive hybrid electrolytes — and in particular the interfacial ion dynamics where solid meets polymer, including grafted and grafted-free polymer interfaces. I study local transport and solvation in these heterogeneous systems through simulation.

Before Münster I earned a Master's in Chemical Engineering from IIT Gandhinagar, modelling supercapacitors with molecular dynamics and experiment, and a Bachelor's from MANIT Bhopal. A spell as a graduate engineer at Vedanta's aluminium and power operations gave me a feel for energy systems at industrial scale.
